About this Blog

This blog is a small notebook for things I want to understand more clearly and remember more carefully.

Some posts will be technical: mathematical derivations, experiments I have tried, algorithms, models, or notes from research and coding. Others may be less formal: reading notes, film reflections, shared knowledge, or fragments from daily life.

I do not want every piece here to be polished into a final answer. I want this space to keep the process of thinking: questions, partial explanations, small observations, and ideas that may become useful later.

Some notes may be incomplete or mistaken. Corrections, questions, and discussions are always welcome.
