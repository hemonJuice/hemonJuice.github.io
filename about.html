About me

Hi, I'm Hanwen Ju. I was born in Changzhou, China in 1999, and currently living in Hamilton, Canada. I am a recent graduate of McMaster University where I earned my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science. I'll be continuing my academic journey as a graduate student at the University of Waterloo, majoring in data science and artificial intelligence in fall 2023. Stepping into the era of data explosion, machine learning has become indispensable in a wide range of areas. As a "fanatics" of data science and statistics, I am particularly fascinated by unsupervised learning, specifically clustering. Outside of my academic pursuits, I am a photographer and a huge fan of contemporary art.
